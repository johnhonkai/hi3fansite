    Fenghuang of Vicissitude

    Fu Hua | Release Date: v7.2 (18 Jan 2024)

    OVERVIEW

    Roles

  • Swift and Powerful Fire DPS: Peak form of Fu Hua. Kicks, punches, divebombs, blasts fireballs and one-punch-nukes enemies. Abilities reduce and recover HP to gain buffs.

    • Pull Recommendation

  • F2P Players: Only use your crystal to pull Part 2 valkyries, since they use a new Astral Ring system. If FoV and her gears are available through other means, such as spending event or Battle Pass, you can consider getting them that way.
  • Competitive: FoV bosses still appear every patch. However, getting FoV should not be prioritized over Part 2 characters. SS-rank has a big upgrade that completely changes rotation.

    • How to Get

  • Fenghuang of Vicissitude: Battlesuit Supply
  • Torch of Eons and Fu Hua: Ordinary Days: Focused Supply

    Check out the full guide on Youtube

    FoV DPS
    DETAILS Herrscher of Truth provides Elemental Breach, strong buffs and more. Chrono Navi provides huge buffs, ignite, sp regen and more.
    EQUIPMENT
    BEST: Torch of Eons + Fu Hua: Ordinary Dayss
  • Affix: Full ATK
  • Torch of Eons: Has buffs, sp recovery and extra nuke.
  • Fu Hua Ordinary Days: You want full set, each piece is strong, 3pc is just busted. Missing one piece is around 20% damage loss, which is a lot for a Part 1 valk.
  • VS G4 Little Joys: TB or MB 2PC Ordinary Days is still stronger than G4 Little Joys.
