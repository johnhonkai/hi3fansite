    Fenghuang of Vicissitude

    Fu Hua | Release Date: v7.2 (18 Jan 2024)

    OVERVIEW

    Roles

  • Swift and Powerful Fire DPS: Peak form of Fu Hua. Kicks, punches, divebombs, blasts fireballs and one-punch-nukes enemies. Abilities reduce and recover HP to gain buffs.

    • Pull Recommendation

  • F2P Players: Only use your crystal to pull Part 2 valkyries, since they use a new Astral Ring system. If FoV and her gears are available through other means, such as spending event or Battle Pass, you can consider getting them that way.
  • Competitive: FoV bosses still appear every patch. However, getting LV should not be prioritized over Part 2 characters. SS-rank has a big upgrade that completely changes rotation.

    • How to Get

  • Fenghuang of Vicissitude: Battlesuit Supply
  • Torch of Eons and Fu Hua: Ordinary Days: Focused Supply

    HOW TO PLAY
  • 1. Use weapon to enter Frenzy mode
  • 2. Press evade > Press and hold Basic ATK to use chainsaw slash
  • 3. At the end of slash sequence, Basic ATK button will flash, press Basic ATK to cast Combo ATK
  • 4. Repeat Step 2 and 3
  • Use Ultimate whenever available
  • If the battle takes too long, Elemental Breach and Lightning DMG buff will expire (Luna icon above HP bar). Hold weapon to end Frenzy mode, then when meter is full again, use weapon to activate Frenzy mode.
