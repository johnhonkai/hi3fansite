    OVERVIEW

    Roles

  • World Star Support: When fully geared, Helia is a powerful support for World Star team (Deepspace Anchor and Schicksal's Imperative).

    • Farm Recommendation

  • F2P Players: Not a priortiy, but you should farm Helia if you have Deepspace Anchor or Schicksal's Imperative, and is lacking another support for the valk.
  • Competitive:
    - For Senadina DPS, Helia is still one of the best supports especially at S0-rank, BUT Helia can be swapped out for another S-rank for more timestop and self dmg (higher score).
    - SIMP has higher resonance trigger limit, so she does not need to rely on Helia's resonance mark for damage.
  • Electrifying Arc: Although Helia does not rely on Electrifying Arc to function, this weapon contributes around 20% of team dmg, which is big. Electrifying Arc is free from Part 2 Chapter 5,

    • How to Get

  • Helia: Asterite Shop, Open World Task, Supply
  • Electrifying Arc: Part 2 Chapter 5, Equipment Supply, Synergy Upgrade in Asterite Shop
  • Von Neumann: Equipment Supply, Forge

    HOW TO PLAY
    1. Activate Stellar Outburst
    2. Press weapon to enter Rapid Shot state, then just let Helia fire and auto-cast grenade
    3. To recover meter, press weapon, then Basic ATK to launch follow-up attack
    4. If you have Ego signet [Meditation], you can use Ult to recover full meter
    5. Repeat steps 2,3,4 until Stellar Outburst ends
    6. During AR Charging state, just do step 2 and 3 until AR is full again
    7. Repeat
