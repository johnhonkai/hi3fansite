    Lone Destruction: Shadowchaser

    Tsavorae 'Lantern' | Release Date: v7.5 (6 June 2024)

    SUPPORT FOR
    OVERVIEW

    Roles

  • Gunslinger Fire DPS: Switches between three firearms to attack. Has hight hitcount. No ignite.
  • World Star Support: Triggers resonance marks. For Sena and Simp support, Lantern is less optimal than other options. Lantern's buffs focus more on Fire DMG buff, but we don't have any Fire Resonance DPS right now.
  • WoDestiny Support: One of the best supports for all WoDestiny DPS so far. As Jovial Deception support, Lantern provides 30% Crit Rate and 30-35% Crit DMG.

    • Pull Recommendation

  • F2P Players: Although Lantern is a great unit, so is every other Part 2 S-rank. Not highly recommended to get Lantern due to Lantern having less value than other Part 2 S-ranks.
  • Competitive: Recommended to pull as DPS, and as support for Thelema and Jovial Deception.

    • How to Get

  • Lantern and Weapon: Supply
  • Stigma: Supply, Forgeable
