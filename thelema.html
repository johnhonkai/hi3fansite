    Mad Pleasure: Shadowbringer

    Thelema Nutrisco | Release Date: v7.4 (25 April 2024)

    SUPPORT FOR
    OVERVIEW

    ROLES

  • DPS: Powerful Ice DPS with shieldbreak and huge freeze trauma.
  • WS and WoD Support: Provide strong buffs with important utilities: Shieldbreak, freeze trauma, AR Regen and timestop.
  • Timestop: Extended timestop means better score as both dps and support.
  • AR Regen: Has a unique infinite aerial jump evasion tech. When paired with signature weapon's 2 AR regen on evasion, Thelema can give the team a lot of AR regen in a short time.

    • PULL RECOMMENDATION

  • F2P Players: Recommended, but not mandatory for the average player.
  • Competitive: Highly recommended. If you can afford it, get S2-rank for a major upgrade in rotation and damage.

    • HOW TO GET

  • Thelema and Weapon: Supply
  • Stigma: Supply, Forgeable

    HOW TO PLAY
  • Activate Stellar Outburst.
  • Use Support (phantom) to get Rondo-in-the-Shadows buff.
  • Spam Basic ATK. This recovers Banquet, and will auto-cast Moment of Indulgence when Banquet is full.
  • To further optimize Basic ATK, when Basic ATK SEQ 3 hits, press evade then Basic ATK quickly to do animation cancelling.
  • Use weapon skill once to gather enemies or to activate weapon-triggered buffs like Helix or Stars signet.
