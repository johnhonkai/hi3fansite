    Lone Planetfarer

    Vita | Release Date: v7.8 (17 Oct 2024)

    SUPPORT FOR
    OVERVIEW

    Roles

  • DPS: Good DPS, but only performs exceptionally well in favorable matchups.
  • WS, WoD, ??? Supports: Vita is a powerful support for World Star, WoDestiny, and one future AR team. Vita provides Ele/Phy Breach, buffs and higher AR regen.
  • The other 9 AR Supports: Vita can also support the other 9 Astral Ring teams. Vita still provides Breach and buffs, but at a reduced effectiveness.

    • Pull Recommendation

  • New Players / Have not played since 7.3: Recommended to pull Vita for her flexible support capability. As DPS, her support options are very limited in 7.8 (until a new RoO valk is added), so new players won't be able to use her optimal DPS team.
  • F2P, already have Senadina: Vita is not a top priority, since you already have Senadina to support World Star and WoDestiny teams. The only thing Sena cannot do is supporting the other 9 AR teams.
  • Competitive: Recommended, Vita's value is insane.
    - For World Star team, currently Vita is undeniably the best support.
    - For WoDestiny team, S0-Vita loses to S0-Sena (small score difference). At higher ranks and synergy, Vita beats Sena.
    - For the future ??? AR team, Vita is the uncontested best support, at least until we get another S-rank support for that AR.

    • How to Get

  • Vita and Weapon: Supply
  • Stigma: Equipment Supply, Forgeable, Free stigma box in 7.8
