    Herrscher of Finality

    Kiana Kaslana | Release Date: v6.4 (16 Feb 2023)

    DLC Date: v7.8 (17 Oct 2024)

    SUPPORT FOR
    OVERVIEW

    Roles

  • DPS: Powerful Fire-IMG DPS, the culmination of 6 years of journey. HoFi's full potential is only unlocked in Herrscher Trio team with Herrscher of Origin and Herrscher of Truth.
  • Rite of Oblivion DPS: When HoFi equips the new Divine Key Waxing Moon, she unlocks Astral Ring: Rite of Oblivion. HoFi's role also expands into a support for the following teams:
  • Vita Support: In 7.8, HoFi+DLC is an important support for the new valk Vita [Lone Planetfarer]. Without HoFi, Vita loses around 50% DMG, which is massive, and at this point, even Vita support performs better than Vita DPS. This will remain true until we get a new Rite-of-Oblivion character.
  • World Star Support: Can trigger Domain Resonance marks 6 times.
  • HoOrigin Support: Iskandar stigma has buffs for HoOrigin DPS. However, HoOrigin top score bosses have not appeared so far in live version.
  • General AR Support: As a RoOblivion character, HoFi can technically support other AR teams, but she is as terrible as Coralie.

    • Pull Recommendation

  • New Players on HoFi: In 7.8, HoFi is available in spending event shop. You need to spend 44,800 crystal to get enough tokens for HoFi card. If you have enough crystals, and you are pulling Vita, then it is recommended to try hitting this milestone by pulling Vita, Vita's gears, and HoFi's DLC gears. If you don't have enough crystal, then it's not recommended to get HoFi's DLC gears.
  • Competitive Players on DLC Gears: Recommended to get, especially for Vita support and World Star support. HoOrigin support is more of a bonus. As DPS, HoFi DPS should be more useful for 2-3 patches, since her bosses should return more often now that she gets a DLC.
  • For Super Whales: Waxing Moon pistol synergy (duplicates) gives global ATK (Syn1 +10 ATK, Syn2 +5 ATK, Syn3 +5 ATK).
  • Original Gears: No reason to get them now.

    • How to Get

  • Herrscher of Finality: 7.8 Spending Event, Battlesuit Supply
  • Waxing Moon: Equipment Supply
  • Iskandar Stigma: Equipment Supply, Forge, Free Box in 7.8
  • Domain of Genesis and Kiana Kaslana stigma: Focused Supply

    • EQUIPMENT
    BEST: Waxing Moon + Iskandar
  • Best set for both dps and support roles.
  • Waxing Moon: A must-have for HoFi: Huge dmg buff with important utilties:
    1. Enables Astral Ring system for HoFi. HoFi gains AR Rite of Oblivion, as well as Omniscient Star (RoOblivion) and Domain Resonance (WorldStar) traits.
    2. Similar to Domain of Genesis, Waxing Moon allows HoFi to quickly gain Descent points, massively improves rotation.
    3. Gives access to a new, stronger, cooler Ultimate.
    4. Synergy (duplicates) gives global ATK (Syn1 +10 ATK, Syn2 +5 ATK, Syn3 +5 ATK).

  • Iskandar: Has DPS and support buffs. Requires Waxing Moon to activate most of the effects.
    Q: Can I use Iskandar on other valks or HoFi with Domain of Genesis?
    A: No, the stigma effects specifically require HoFi with Waxing Moon.
    SP Regen: Iskandar only gives sp regen after you use Ult (20 sp) and after activating Stellar Outburst (10 sp), while Kiana Kaslana stigma gives 16 sp during rotation.
    Affix: Full ATK

    • OLD: Domain of Genesis + Kiana Kaslana
  • Old equipment. As DPS, you can use Kiana Kaslana with Waxing Moon pistol, but there is no reason to, because it's just worse than Iskandar.
  • Affix: Full ATK.
