    Lunar Vow: Crimson Love

    Luna 'Theresa' | Release Date: v7.0 (26 Oct 2023)

    SUPPORT FOR
    OVERVIEW

    Roles

  • DPS: One of the strongest Part 1 valk when fully geared. Luna's attacks count as Bleed DMG, so she can benefit from both Bleed and Lightning buffs.
  • Support: As a Part 1 Lightning support, Luna provides Elemental Breach, sp regen and contributes team dmg herself.

    • Pull Recommendation

  • F2P Players: Only use your crystal to pull Part 2 valkyries, since they use a new Astral Ring system. If LV and her gears are available through other means, such as spending event or Battle Pass, you can consider getting them that way.
  • Competitive: Her scores are now even higher with the new Schicksal's Imperative support. However, getting LV should not be prioritized over Part 2 characters.

    • How to Get

  • Lunar Vow: Battlesuit Supply
  • Bloodied Casket and Sweet Dreams stigma: Focused Supply

    DETAILS

    • Part 1 Lightning Support
    EQUIPMENT
    BEST: Bloodied Casket + Sweet Dreams
  • Best set for both dps and support roles.
  • Affix: Lunar Vow uses Lightning DMG affix. The reason is LV's attack multipliers are very low but has high hitcount.
  • Bloodied Casket: Pure damage for Lunar Vow. No utility except for the Bleed trauma per second. In Elysian Realm, weapon active also allows you to benefit from Infinity signet.
  • Sweet Dreams: Has dps and support buffs. 3PC has sp regen.
    • ELYSIAN REALM

    Basic ATK Playstyle

    Lunar Vow stays at very low HP to deal massive damage. Don't worry, she gains a permanent shield and has dmg resistance, so you won't die that easily.

    HOW TO PLAY
  • 1. Use weapon to enter Frenzy mode
  • 2. Press evade > Press and hold Basic ATK to use chainsaw slash
  • 3. At the end of slash sequence, Basic ATK button will flash, press Basic ATK to cast Combo ATK
  • 4. Repeat Step 2 and 3
  • Use Ultimate whenever available
  • If the battle takes too long, Elemental Breach and Lightning DMG buff will expire (Luna icon above HP bar). Hold weapon to end Frenzy mode, then when meter is full again, use weapon to activate Frenzy mode.
