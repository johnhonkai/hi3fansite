    Valkyrie Blastmetal

    Coralie 6626 Planck | Release Date: v7.3 (29 Feb 2024)

    SUPPORT FOR
    OVERVIEW

    Roles

  • Free-to-Play Part 2 Support: Valkyrie Blastmetal is a free-to-play support option for any Astral Ring team.
  • World Star Support: Can trigger Domain Resonance marks.
  • Vita Support: In 7.8, Coralie is a mandatory support for the new valkyrie Vita [Lone Planetfarer], until a better option is added.

    • Farm Recommendation

  • F2P Players: Recommended to farm Coralie since she can be deployed in any Astral Ring team, especially if you are new and missing an S-rank for the third member in the team. Plus, all her equipment are free.
  • Competitive: Coralie is a mandatory support for Vita in v7.8. When a new AR type is introduced, and the AR DPS lacks a proper S-rank support, then Coralie will become useful again.

    • How to Get

  • Coralie: Part 2 Chapter 1, Asterite Shop, Open World Task, Supply
  • Blast Hammer: Part 2 Chapter 1, Part 2 Chapter 5, Synergy Upgrade in Asterite Shop
  • Tsiolkovsky: Equipment Supply, Forge
