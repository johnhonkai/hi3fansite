    Jovial Deception: Shadowdimmer

    Songque 'Maria' | Release Date: v7.6 (25 July 2024)

    SUPPORT FOR
    OVERVIEW

    Roles

  • DPS: Powerful ranged SD Physical DPS with a unique ability to instantly trigger an SD enemy's stardust explosion. JD can also end Stellar Outburst early and refund AR meter, making her rotation more flexible.
  • VS Senadina: Against SD enemies, JD is better against solo boss. Against swarm enemies like Bloodthirsty SD-Mech mobs, it depends on ranks- or you can use JD and Sena on the same team.
  • WoDestiny Support: Provides strong buffs, but currently not the best support for any WoD DPS. Stellar Drain is slow.
  • Future AR Support: Has a locked skill related to a future Astral Ring.

    • Pull Recommendation

  • F2P Players: JD is recommended IF you have tons of spare crystals AFTER you get Vita. JD will cover the annoying Physical and PhyShield weather enemies, and she will also be a support for a future AR team.
  • Competitive: The instant SD explosion is huge, and JD is also not bad as a Physical / Ranged valk, and topscores Ranged Kalpas. In addition, she will also be the uncontested best support for a future AR team, at least until another S-rank support is added for that AR.

    • How to Get

  • Jovial Deception and Weapon: Supply
  • Stigma: Supply, Forgeable

    DETAILS Jovial Deception lineup has a bit of complexity to it.
    Crit Rate: Serapeum AstralOP ensures that all JD attacks will crit. Without the Crit conversion from Serapeum, Lantern is more valuable since she provides 30% Crit Rate. However, for most casual players, Lantern is not mandatory, since most of JD's important attacks (Ult, Combo, Weapon) already have boosted Crit Rate.
    Elemental Breach Conversion: JD has a unique passive: When JD has Elemental Breach, if Crit Rate > 100%, gain Physical DMG +33%. If Crit Rate < 100%, gain 22% Crit Rate and TDM +22%. Currently, the only Part 2 supports that can provide Elemental Breach are Senadina, and the upcoming Lone Planetfarer.
    Fastest opener: With SS-rank Serapeum AstralOp, Thelema + Lantern supports provide the fastest opener to get Stellar Outburst on first rotation. This only matters for Top 50 players.
    Support Specializations: Thelema provides Shieldbreak, while Lantern provides hitcount shredder.

    HOW TO PLAY
  • Activate Stellar Outburst.
  • Use Support (phantom).
  • Spam Basic ATK until Combo ATK meter is full, then use Combo ATK. Repeat until SO is almost over.
  • When Stellar Outburst is almost over, use Ultimate.
  • In AR Charging state, press Basic ATK > Weapon > Combo ATK. Stellar Outburst should be available again.
