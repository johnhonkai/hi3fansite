    Deepspace Anchor: First Light

    Senadina | Release Date: v7.3 (29 Feb 2024)

    SUPPORT FOR
    OVERVIEW

    Roles

  • DPS: Good Lightning SD DPS. Excels against SD mobs thanks to dmg link mechanic. Does not beat Jovial Deception vs solo SD boss, or Lunar Vow vs lightning boss, BUT can still handle them well enough for the average player.
  • WoDestiny Support: Provides Elemental Breach and strong buffs. Has zero on-field time.
  • World Star Support: Can support SIMP, but less effective than Vita or HoFi.
  • Songque Synergy: When Sena is paired with Songque AstralOp, activates the passive [Initial AR +25].

    • Pull Recommendation

  • All Players: I think captains are specifically wondering who to pull between Vita or Sena.
    - For WoD team: At S-rank, Sena beats Vita. At SS+ ranks, Vita starts beating Sena's scores.
    - For WS team: Vita is always better than Sena for SIMP dps.
    - General AR Support: One of the main reasons to prioritize pulling Vita over Sena is her ability to support future AR teams (Best support for one AR, less effective for the other 9 AR teams).
  • Based on the reasons listed above, pulling Sena in 7.8 is not highly recommended.

  • Senadina and Weapon: Supply
  • Stigma: Supply, Forgeable

    EQUIPMENT
    BEST: Red Star Thunderbolt + Perfect Morning
  • 1. Affix: Full ATK.
  • 2. Perfect Morning: MID and 3PC have WoDestiny support buffs that require Sena to consume 30 sp on standby (off field). The sp consumption is triggered when battle starts, when SO activates, and when SO ends. If Sena is on the field when enemy spawns (battle starts), Sena does consume sp but SHE IS NOT ON STANDBY, so the stigma buffs will not activate.
  • 3. Perfect Morning: When supporting WoDestiny team, if Sena switches in, some of the buffs from [Custom Module: Radon] will be disabled permanently. However, if Sena equips Perfect Morning 3PC, Senadina can switch in without losing any buff.

  • START: Evade > Basic ATK x2 > Weapon > Basic ATK > Weapon x2 (Thunder in Clear Skies) (If you don't know what to press, just follow the flashing buttons.)

  • If weapon button doesn't flash: Evade > Basic ATK x2 > Weapon x2
  • Use Ultimate whenever available.

    • NOTE
  • AR meter carries over from floor to floor. If the battle ends during Stellar Outburst, Stellar Outburst will also end early and you keep the remaining AR meter.
  • Be careful not to activate Stellar Outburst after the battle is over. This will reset your AR meter to zero.

    Stellar Outburst Playstyle

  • A playstyle centered around buffing Domain Resonance damage.
  • Sena has a weird issue compared to other Part 2 valks: SHE DOES NOT START THE FLOOR WITH FULL AR METER and NONE OF HER SIGNET GIVE AR METER AFTER STELLAR OUTBURST ENDS. Instead, AR meter carries over from floor to floor. This means you are forced to play regular Senadina while waiting for AR meter to fill up.
  • Another way to do this playstyle is to start with [Initial Dawning] to make AR Charging state stronger, and then get the SO-related signets on Floor 7 using Because of You sigil.
