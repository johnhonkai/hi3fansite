    Schicksal's Imperative

    Theresa Apocalypse | Release Date: September 5, 2024 (v7.7)
    ASTRAL RING
    SUPPORT FOR

    OVERVIEW

  • Qua DPS: Has auto quantum collapse shield. Can consume teammates resonance marks during Stellar Outburst.
  • World Star - WoDestiny Support: Just another buffer and resonance triggerer, nothing special besides huge paralyze trauma and auto quantum collapse.
  • Lunar Vow Support: Gives Elemental Breach (replaces HoTr), huge paralyze trauma (replaces Eden, or can use them both) and faster rotation (just ult and sleep).

    • PULL RECOMMENDATION

  • F2P Players: Not recommended because v7.8 is a CN anniversary patch with big spending event.
  • Competitive: SIMP topscores the new qua boss Varaha, and a big upgrade to Lunar Vow.

    • HOW TO GET

  • Teri and Chainblade: Supply
  • Stigma: Supply, Forgeable, Free stigma box in 7.7

    Check out the full guide on Hoyolab or Youtube
