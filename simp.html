    Schicksal's Imperative

    Theresa Apocalypse | Release Date: v7.7 (5 Sep 2024)

    SUPPORT FOR
    OVERVIEW

    Roles

  • DPS:
    - As a Quantum DPS, SIMP has multiple ways to apply quantum collapse.
    - As a Resonance DPS, Homu's domain resonance marks can be triggered 18 times. SIMP can also consumes teammate's marks.
    - As a Part 1 DPS, SIMP can create a double dps team SIMP x Lunar Vow. It has been used against Paralyze Sushang and Starless Benares.
  • World Star Support: Mark triggerer + good buffs + has timestop. Currently in 7.8, we only have Sena as resonance dps, and there is not enough bosses to evaluate SIMP's effectiveness vs the new supports Vita and HoFi.
  • WoDestiny Support: Good support, can just ult and stay in the back like Senadina. However, SIMP does not offer any improvement on existing WoDestiny teams.
  • Lunar Vow Support: Gives Elemental Breach (replaces HoTr), huge paralyze trauma (replaces Eden) and faster rotation (just ult and sleep).
  • Part 1 Lightning Support: SIMP provides Total DMG buffs, Paralyze trauma and sp regen.
  • Other Support: Technically, since SIMP provides Total DMG, you can use her to support other elements that require Paralyze trauma or quantum collapse.

    • Pull Recommendation

  • F2P Players: Not highly recommended.
  • Competitive: SIMP topscores quite a number of bosses qua boss Valrahal, resonance Opaque Shadow, Paralyze Sushang, and Starless Benares. SIMP is also a big upgrade to Lunar Vow.

    • How to Get

  • Teri and Chainblade: Supply
  • Stigma: Supply, Forgeable.

    EQUIPMENT
    BEST: Vacation Notice + Day After Day Set
  • Vacation Notice: Gives more Stress meter and more Paralyze trauma. Team gains a shield, upon being hit by a quantum collapse attack, apply quantum collapse.
  • Day After Day: Best for most situations / general purpose. There are better options for supporting Thelema / Lantern, and as Part 1 DPS.
    • SITUATIONAL: Bronya Zaychik set
  • When supporting Thelema or Lantern, Bronya Zaychik stigma provides around 5% more dmg per rotation than Day After Day set.
    • PART 1 DPS: Zeno TM DayAfterDay B
  • When SIMP is a Part 1 DPS, Zeno TM provides 18% more dmg than Day After Day set.
    • TRANSITIONAL: Banquet Rose
